This IQ DATAFIELD connects a Forumslader to your Garmin bike computer.

It connects to a Forumslader V5 or V6 (a generator-based battery/charge controller for
e-bikes and dynamo-powered bikes) via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), and displays/records its
live telemetry - battery status, charge/discharge current, dynamo output, speed, distance,
and more - directly on your Garmin cycling computer. The data field connects automatically,
keeps reconnecting in the background if the BLE link drops, and requires no companion phone
app.

FEATURES
========

Display Fields:
The following Forumslader metrics can be assigned to any of the 4 display fields (see
"Configure Display Fields" below):
- Trip Energy (Wh), Temperature (deg C, if sensor installed), Dynamo Power (W),
  Generator Gear, Distance/Odometer, Battery Current (A, signed), Electrical Load (W),
  Speed, Battery Level (%), Day/Tour Distance (resettable), Consumed Energy (Wh, since
  activity start), Estimated Range (projected, based on consumption/distance since
  activity start), BLE Diagnostic Dashboard (see below)

Depending on the "Scroll Fields" setting, either all 4 configured fields are shown
concatenated on one line separated by a middle dot (·), or one field at a time, cycling on
tap.

Battery/charge indicator:
A small battery icon (with a charging-bolt overlay while charging) is drawn in the corner
of the data field once a Forumslader is connected, reflecting capacity and charging state.

BLE Diagnostic Dashboard:
Real-time BLE connection diagnostics, useful for troubleshooting an unstable connection:
- D: Disconnect Count since the data field started
- E: Checksum Errors (payload) since the data field started
- B: BLE Throughput (bytes/s currently received)

Battery Capacity Calculation:
Computed either from coulomb counting (accumulated charge/discharge current, more accurate
but requires an initial full-charge calibration) or from battery voltage (simpler, less
precise) - selectable via the "Coulomb Count" setting.

Alerts:
When enabled, on-screen alerts are raised for: Battery Level Below 20%, Short Circuit,
System Interrupt.

Auto Trip Reset:
When enabled, the Forumslader's trip counters are automatically reset once the connected
activity's timer starts, so every activity starts with a clean trip energy baseline without
a manual reset on the Forumslader itself.

Lock to Device:
When enabled, the app remembers and reconnects only to the specific Forumslader it last
paired with, instead of the first matching device found - useful if several
Forumslader-equipped bikes may be nearby.

Recording (FIT):
All display fields can additionally be recorded to your activity's FIT file via 4
independently configurable Recording Fields, so you can review telemetry later in Garmin
Connect or any FIT-compatible tool - even for metrics not shown on the data field itself.

Configure Display and Recording Fields:
1. Open the app settings menu (gear icon) on the Garmin, or use Garmin Connect Mobile App
2. Select "Show Fields" or "Recording Fields"
3. Choose any Forumslader metric for each field (or "Off" to leave it unused)

Connectivity:
The app scans for and connects automatically once the data field is active - no manual
pairing needed. If the BLE connection drops after a device was connected, the app waits for
automatic reconnection rather than rescanning immediately; otherwise it falls back to an
active rescan with increasing backoff between attempts.

Forumslader V5 & V6 are supported. Older versions are not compatible with Garmin devices.
V6 devices older than 08/2023 need a firmware update, downloadable via the Forumslader App.
Requires Forumslader App v2.05+: https://www.forumslader.de/forumslader-pro/technik/bluetooth-v6/

IMPORTANT:
Make sure the Forumslader is not concurrently connected to a smartphone with Forumslader App
